About DrupalCamp 2019

We are happy to announce the first-ever Drupal Camp in Chennai organized by the Drupal Chennai Community. Drupal Camp Chennai 2019 is a conference organized for the Drupal community and people showing interest in Drupal. This two-day event featuring training workshops and sessions will be held 7th and 8th December 2019 in IIT Madras. The Drupalcamp is not only about the conference, the sessions and the informal gatherings (BOFs). After all, you come to Drupal for the code and stay for the community. The conference programme has been arranged to provide every participant with what they need – both people making first steps in Drupal and advanced developers. During the conference, speakers will deliver their lectures in native-languages of South India like Tamil, Malayalam, Telugu and English. Spend your weekend with a wonderful community which collaborates, networks, connects, and shares. The community that is Drupal is truly exceptional.
